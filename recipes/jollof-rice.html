Jollof Rice

Description

The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat in a single pot, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions.

ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Cooking Oil
  • Fish

Steps

  • Add parboiled rice to a pot
  • Mix it very well with the tomato stew
  • Add any condiments of your choice
  • Cook on medium to low heat for 15-30 minutes
